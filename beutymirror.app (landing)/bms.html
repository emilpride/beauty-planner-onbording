What is BMS?

BMS is our original holistic metric. It combines several aspects of your well-being into a single score, making it easier to understand your balance between beauty and health.

Skin Health

Analyzes clarity, elasticity, hydration, and overall condition of your skin.

Hair Health

Evaluates thickness, shine, and resilience of your hair.

Mental Health

Reflects stress levels, emotional stability, and overall psychological balance.

Physical Health

Includes fitness, sleep quality, nutrition, and body vitality.

How We Calculate BMS

Each component has its own weight in the final index:

Skin Health – 30%

Skin is the first indicator of overall well-being, so it plays the biggest role.

Hair Health – 20%

Healthy hair reflects nutrition, hormones, and external care.

Mental Health – 25%

Emotional stability and reduced stress are essential for harmony.

Physical Health – 25%

A strong body supports both external beauty and mental balance.

FAQ About BMS

Is BMS scientific?

BMS is a conceptual, holistic index that uses real health and beauty metrics but presents them in an accessible, easy-to-read form.

Can my score change?

Yes. Your BMS is dynamic and can improve with lifestyle changes, self-care, and health management.

Is it only about looks?

No. Unlike beauty scores, BMS emphasizes overall harmony — inner and outer.
